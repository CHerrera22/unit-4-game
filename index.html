THE CRYSTALS COLLECTOR

How to Play

At the start of the game, you will be given a random number goal. Each crystal will also be assigned a random number value. Collect the crystals to accumulate points toward your random number goal. Extrapolate the value of each crystal based on the points you earn. Each new game will populate new values.

Match your crystal points to your number goal EXACTLY to win. If you go over the goal, you lose.
